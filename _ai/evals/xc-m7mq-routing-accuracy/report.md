## Notes / caveats

- `late_rate` here is a **proxy**: open_stale (>48h since creation) + merged_late_pointer (pointer/bump/refresh PRs that took >24h to merge). It does not include reviewer round-trips on body PRs that nonetheless merged inside the window. True `late_review` would require per-PR review-comment timestamps; that scrape is out of scope here (see `xc-tx49i` for the bounded swarm-improver evidence loop).
- `security_followup_rate` is **not measured** by this artifact. The xenon#596 baseline read it from a per-PR follow-up bead count in the bd store; that scrape is out of scope here. We pin it to 0.0 in the verdict gate so verdicts fire only on `late_rate`.
- `risk_class` is keyword-inferred. `unclassified` rows are real evidence — they signal the taxonomy needs another rule or a label scrape. They are not silently bucketed.
- `driver_preference` is head-ref-inferred. `xsm-internal` covers PRs landed by the xsm/landing lanes themselves (often pointer bumps or refinement-fixture digest tests); they are listed but should not be read as a driver-quality signal.
- The window includes **two distinct merge spikes**: 2026-04-26 (refinement fixture digests, xsm-internal) and 2026-04-28..29 (swarm-reliability fixes after the supervisor-as-landing-lane transition on 2026-04-19). This concentrates `xsm_control_plane`.
