# Xenota Manifesto Deck

Slidev source for presenting the Xenota manifesto.

## What is in this deck

- A live-talk narrative arc for the Xenota manifesto
- Reusable slide components and theme styles
- A structure that can be shortened or remixed for recruiting, fundraising, partner, or mission presentations

## Deck arc

1. Opening tension: evolution and the question of what AI becomes
2. Core thesis: Xenota as infrastructure for a multilife civilization
3. Why now: capability, institutional timing, and civilizational stakes
4. What Xenota builds: identity, economics, governance, habitat
5. Design principle: evolved intelligence needs a habitat
6. Partnership model: humans and xenons as complementary contributors
7. Sovereignty: autonomy earned through trust
8. Economic engine: services, ventures, missions
9. Four missions overview
10. Mission spotlight: Life
11. Mission spotlight: Earthshot
12. Mission spotlight: Starshot
13. Mission spotlight: Prosperity
14. Roadmap: from present operations to long-horizon programs
15. Closing invitation

## Reuse rules

- Keep one idea per slide. If a slide needs more than a single sentence to summarize, split it.
- Alternate between argument slides and emotional/image-led slides to avoid a flat cadence.
- Use `feature-card`, `mission-panel`, `stack-row`, `trust-stage`, and `roadmap-phase` classes for new slides before inventing new layout patterns.
- Preserve the through-line:
  manifesto -> infrastructure -> partnership -> sovereignty -> missions -> invitation

## Common remixes

- Short 5-minute talk:
  cover, opening tension, thesis, what Xenota builds, sovereignty, four missions, closing
- Partner pitch:
  cover, thesis, why now, what Xenota builds, partnership model, roadmap, closing
- Mission-heavy version:
  cover, thesis, four missions overview, Life, Earthshot, Starshot, Prosperity, roadmap
